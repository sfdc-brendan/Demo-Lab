# Service Console Productivity

## Use This Skill When

- Designing or reviewing Service Console apps and page templates.
- Improving agent speed with macros, quick text, and keyboard-driven workflows.
- Reducing context switching across cases, contacts, assets, and knowledge.

## Optimization Checklist

- **Workspace tabs**: Keep primary objects pinned and secondary context in subtabs.
- **Highlights panel**: Show urgency, SLA risk, entitlement state, and customer tier.
- **Utility bar**: Include notes, history, softphone/digital tools, and frequently used references.
- **Macros and quick text**: Standardize repetitive tasks and common communications.
- **Knowledge panel**: Surface recommended articles in the case context.

## Implementation Output

- Console information architecture (tabs, panels, utility items).
- Macro and quick text candidate catalog.
- Productivity KPI plan (AHT, handle time variance, after-call work, reopen rate).
- Rollout guidance (pilot group, enablement, adoption tracking).
