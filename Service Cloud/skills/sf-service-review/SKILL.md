# Service Cloud Review

## Use This Skill When

- Reviewing Service Cloud architecture or implementation readiness.
- Performing health checks before deployment or rollout.
- Prioritizing remediation with severity and business impact.

## 200-Point Rubric

- Case management model: 40
- Omni-Channel routing and staffing: 40
- Entitlements and SLA controls: 30
- Knowledge and deflection readiness: 30
- Console productivity and UX: 20
- Voice/digital channel operations: 20
- Governance, metrics, and change adoption: 20

## Review Output Format

1. Overall score and risk level.
2. Findings by severity: critical, high, medium, low.
3. Top 5 fixes by business impact and effort.
4. Rollout and measurement recommendations.
