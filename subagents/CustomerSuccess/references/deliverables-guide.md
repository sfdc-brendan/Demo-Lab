# CSM Deliverables Guide

Detailed templates for the seven core Customer Success deliverable types. Each template is designed for reuse across customers, industries, and Salesforce footprints.

---

## Success Plan Template

```markdown
# Success Plan: [Customer Name]
**CSM:** [Name]
**Executive Sponsor (Customer):** [Name / Title]
**Executive Sponsor (Internal):** [Name / Title]
**Contract Anniversary:** [Date]
**Plan Period:** [Start] – [End]
**Status:** [Draft / Active / Complete]
**Last Updated:** [Date]

## 1. Customer Overview
### Business Context
[One paragraph: what the customer does, their strategic priorities this year, and why
Salesforce matters to those priorities.]

### Salesforce Footprint
| Cloud / Product | Edition | Licensed Users | Go-Live Date | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Sales Cloud] | [Enterprise] | [250] | [2024-01] | [Production] |
| [Service Cloud] | [Enterprise] | [100] | [2024-06] | [Production] |
| [Data Cloud] | [—] | [—] | [—] | [Not Purchased] |

### Key Stakeholders
| Name | Role | Engagement Level | Communication Preference |
|---|---|---|---|
| [Name] | [VP Sales] | [Executive Sponsor] | [Monthly 1:1] |
| [Name] | [SF Admin] | [Day-to-Day Champion] | [Weekly Slack] |
| [Name] | [CIO] | [Informed] | [Quarterly QBR only] |

## 2. Business Objectives & Success Metrics
| # | Business Objective | Success Metric | Baseline | Target | Timeline |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | [Reduce case resolution time] | [Avg handle time] | [48 hrs] | [24 hrs] | [Q3 2025] |
| 2 | [Increase rep productivity] | [Opportunities created/rep/month] | [8] | [12] | [Q4 2025] |
| 3 | [Improve customer visibility] | [360-degree view adoption] | [30%] | [80%] | [Q2 2026] |

## 3. Adoption Targets by Phase
### Phase 1: Foundation (Months 1–3)
- [ ] All licensed users logged in at least once
- [ ] Core workflows documented and trained
- [ ] Data migration validated by business owners
- [ ] Executive sponsor briefed on baseline metrics

### Phase 2: Proficiency (Months 4–6)
- [ ] 70%+ weekly active user rate
- [ ] Key automations live and adopted
- [ ] First ROI data point captured
- [ ] Champion network identified (3+ power users per department)

### Phase 3: Optimization (Months 7–12)
- [ ] 85%+ adoption on core features
- [ ] Advanced features enabled (Einstein, automation, reporting)
- [ ] Self-service reporting adopted by business leaders
- [ ] Expansion opportunity assessment complete

## 4. Milestones
| Milestone | Target Date | Owner | Status | Evidence of Completion |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Go-live stabilization complete] | [Date] | [CSM + Admin] | [Complete] | [Zero P1 issues for 2 weeks] |
| [Phase 1 adoption targets met] | [Date] | [CSM] | [In Progress] | [Adoption scorecard review] |
| [First QBR delivered] | [Date] | [CSM + AE] | [Scheduled] | [QBR deck + action items] |

## 5. Communication Cadence
| Audience | Format | Frequency | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Executive Sponsor | 1:1 call or email | Monthly | CSM |
| Champion / Admin | Working session | Weekly | CSM |
| Account Team (internal) | Sync | Bi-weekly | CSM |
| Full stakeholder group | QBR | Quarterly | CSM + AE |

## 6. Risk Register
| Risk | Severity | Leading Indicator | Mitigation | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Low executive engagement] | [High] | [Sponsor missed 2+ meetings] | [Escalate via AE to re-engage] | [CSM] |
| [Data quality issues] | [Medium] | [Duplicate rate > 10%] | [Data cleanup sprint with admin] | [Admin] |

## 7. Expansion Roadmap
| Opportunity | Trigger Event | Timeline | Readiness | Next Step |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| [Data Cloud] | [Customer investing in analytics strategy] | [Q1 2026] | [Medium] | [Discovery session with DSE] |
| [Additional Service Cloud licenses] | [New support center opening] | [Q3 2025] | [High] | [License true-up discussion with AE] |
```

| Attribute | Detail |
|---|---|

| Cloud | [e.g., Sales Cloud] |
| Target User Segment | [e.g., All sales reps — 200 users] |
| Current Adoption | [e.g., 30% (60 of 200 active)] |
| Target Adoption | [e.g., 75% within 30 days] |
| Business Value | [e.g., Eliminates manual activity logging, 2 hrs/rep/week saved] |

## Barrier Diagnosis
| Barrier Type | Present? | Evidence | Severity |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Awareness** | [Yes/No] | [Users don't know the feature exists or what it does] | [High/Med/Low] |
| **Training** | [Yes/No] | [Users tried it but don't know how to use it effectively] | [High/Med/Low] |
| **UX / Usability** | [Yes/No] | [Feature is hard to access or the workflow is confusing] | [High/Med/Low] |
| **Relevance** | [Yes/No] | [Users don't see how it helps their daily work] | [High/Med/Low] |
| **Access / Permissions** | [Yes/No] | [Users don't have the right licenses or permissions] | [High/Med/Low] |
| **Manager Reinforcement** | [Yes/No] | [Managers aren't encouraging or requiring usage] | [High/Med/Low] |
| **Data Quality** | [Yes/No] | [Feature doesn't work well because underlying data is poor] | [High/Med/Low] |
